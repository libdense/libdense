The density wall - naive fanout vs libdense_sim, one CPU core

Steady-state tick time as every player becomes visible to every other in one 24×24 area, Ryzen 5 5600X, single pinned core. Break condition: a design breaks when its steady-state tick exceeds 100 ms - past that no tick rate holds. The naive design scans all entities per client and encodes a per-recipient payload (no fanout reduction); its cost grows with players² and hits the wall at ≈1,300 players. libdense grows linearly and projects to ≈300,000 on the same core.
