# Dense Commercial Licensing

Dense, DenseDB, `libdense_sim`, and associated materials are available under the Dense Community Source License 1.0 for permitted noncommercial, evaluation, and small-commercial uses.

A separate written, paid commercial license is required when the licensee and its Affiliates have aggregate Group Revenue of at least USD $100,000 during the preceding twelve months, or when a proposed use is otherwise outside the Dense Community Source License 1.0.

## Standard Commercial License Fees

The standard annual commercial license fee is determined by the aggregate Group Revenue of the licensee and its Affiliates during the preceding twelve months:

| Aggregate Group Revenue              |                         Annual commercial license fee |
| ------------------------------------ | ----------------------------------------------------: |
| Less than USD $100,000               | No commercial license fee under the revenue threshold |
| USD $100,000 through USD $499,999.99 |                                   USD $1,000 per year |
| USD $500,000 or more                 |                                   USD $5,000 per year |

These are flat annual license fees.

No royalty or percentage of revenue is charged under the standard commercial license.

The applicable fee is based on the highest revenue tier reached during the relevant license year. If the licensee crosses into a higher tier during that year, the licensee must pay the difference between the amount already paid and the fee required for the higher tier.

A commercial agreement may address:

* payment and renewal schedules;
* reporting and revenue-verification requirements;
* OEM and redistribution rights;
* hosted and managed-service rights;
* support and update commitments;
* source-code access;
* warranty, indemnity, and liability terms;
* acquisition and change-of-control treatment; and
* privately negotiated exceptions.

Commercial license fees do not automatically include technical support, integration assistance, custom development, guaranteed updates, service-level commitments, or source-code access unless expressly included in the written commercial agreement.

This notice describes the standard commercial licensing framework only. It is not itself a commercial license, binding offer, or grant of rights.

No commercial rights are granted by this notice alone. Commercial rights exist only after Licensor and the customer execute a separate written commercial license agreement.
